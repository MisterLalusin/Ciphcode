Ultimo-Cifrado is made-up of a three character encryption for each letters in roman alphabet, numbers, and some symbols. The letters and numbers can be decrypted easily by just looking at every third character of the encrypted message.



Example :

normal   - Ultimo-Cifrado
encypted - OUUVWLMNTUAIXYMEIOHPN
           DFCUAIJKFHJRAEAGHDEIO
